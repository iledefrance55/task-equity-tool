Task Equity Tool

Objective Assessment of Task Difficulty

Supporting Fair and Objective Task Evaluation

The Task Equity Tool provides an evidence-based framework for measuring the true difficulty of various tasks, helping ensure fair treatment and appropriate support for all individuals.

This tool is designed to assist healthcare professionals, occupational therapists, disability services, workplace accommodations, and home adaptation specialists in making objective, scientific assessments.

Who Can Benefit

Healthcare Professionals

Occupational therapists, physiotherapists, and rehabilitation specialists can use objective data to develop personalized treatment plans and recommend appropriate accommodations.

Disability Services

Government agencies and disability support organizations can make fair, evidence-based decisions about service allocation and support needs.

Home Adaptation Specialists

Contractors and consultants specializing in accessible home modifications can prioritize improvements based on objective impact assessments.

Legal & Mediation Services

Family mediators, legal professionals, and expert witnesses can use objective data to support fair settlements and custody arrangements regarding household task distribution.

Insurance & Assessment

Insurance evaluators and disability assessors can make evidence-based decisions using standardized, objective measurements rather than subjective judgments.
